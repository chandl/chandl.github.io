About Me

Hi, I’m Chandler Severson and this is my web portfolio. I have a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a Minor in Business Administration from Southern Oregon University. I graduated Magna Cum Laude with a 3.76 GPA in June 2017 at the age of 19. I was born and raised in South Carolina, but attended High School in California starting at the age of 14, when my family moved out to Yreka, a small town in (very) northern California. I was able to skip my sophomore year in high school because of this transition and with the help of dual-enrollment college credit in High School, I was able to skip an entire year of college.


I like video games and programming, and I'm generally a pretty big nerd. In my free time, besides gaming, I enjoy learning new technologies and languages that interest me. Over the course of the last 5 years, I have taught myself Perl, Python, Javascript, Unity3D, and Unreal Engine 4 in my free time. I really enjoy learning about theoretical computer science and have a distinct interest in artificial intelligence and machine learning, which I hope to pursue graduate school for. I really enjoy finding solutions to complicated programs and working through those tough issues with others.
